Introduction of Taiwan

Taiwan is located in between the southeastern coast of the Asian continent and the East Asian islands distributed among the western Pacific Rim. To the north lie Japan and the Ryukyu Islands; to the south are the Philippine Islands; to the west is Mainland China.

Taiwan is endowed with steep mountain ranges; therefore, great altitude differences occur throughout the island. Moreover, a lot of people come to Taiwan for hiking and looking for those beautiful views from the top of the mountains.

Taiwan has 9 national parks and 7 offshore island which are all perfect for vocation. Not just all that vewis and environment stuff, Taiwan is also famous of our night markets, passion from the local people, and our food culture.
